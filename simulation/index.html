Mount the Rail Assembly

Deploy the aluminum T-slot rail system onto the optical breadboard. The horizontal rail controls camera baseline spacing (0–50 cm), while the perpendicular depth rail guides the target (0–10 m).

Select Rail Assembly from the apparatus list → Click Mount Component.

Anodized aluminum T-slot optical rail with linear carriages.
